Movie Database Exercise




Objective

Create an array of movie objects in Javascript. Each movie should have a title, rating, and hasWatched property. Iterate through the array and print out something that looks like:

You have watched "X" - 5 stars
You have not seen "X" - 4.5 stars
You have seen "X" - 3 stars
You have not seen "X" - 2 stars

Feel free to open up your console and call the movieInformation() function to see my solution!
